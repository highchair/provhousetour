Join us for the 39th Festival of Historic Houses! The 2019 House Tour will take place on Saturday and Sunday, June 15–16, in the Paterson Park Neighborhood. Don’t miss your chance to explore the late 19th and early 20th century homes of this tucked-away enclave on Providence’s East Side. 

About the Festival

The 39th Festival of Historic Houses will offer tour-goers the chance to explore the late 19th and early 20th century homes of Providence’s Paterson Park Neighborhood, located in the Blackstone Park Historic District.

More About the Festival

Tour & Tickets

Saturday, June 15th

On Saturday, guests will enjoy self-guided access to lovingly-cared-for private homes, buildings and green spaces throughout the Paterson Park Neighborhood.

Sunday, June 16th

Sunday will feature guided walking tours of the Paterson Park neighborhood and its green spaces from 1 pm - 5 pm.

More Information & Link to Tickets

Preview Party

Thursday, June 13th

A Festival Preview Party will be held on the evening of Thursday, June 13 at Lincoln School’s 1870 Margarethe Dwight House. Your Preview Party ticket includes admission to Saturday’s house tours. Proceeds go toward PPS’s citywide preservation advocacy.
