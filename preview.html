Kickoff Party

Thursday, June 6 from 5:30 – 7:30pm

The 2024 Festival of Historic Houses Kickoff Party is hosted by David Cicilline at his home at 15 Barberry Hill.

The Hazel McCaraby House at 15 Barberry Hill sits on the site of former garages and outbuildings once belonging to the nearby estate at 16 Laurel Avenue. The single-story post-modern house currently on the property was built in 1985 and is a part of the Freeman Plat Historic District. Our host, RI Foundation President David Cicilline, previously served as the mayor of Providence, and congressman for Rhode Island’s 1st congressional district.

Tickets are $165 per person and include admission to Saturday’s self-guided tours
