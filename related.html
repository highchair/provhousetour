James Barnes is a Professor of Architecture at RISD and a registered architect certified by NCARB with an active practice in the southern New England region. His professional work has been by recognized by the RI Chapter AIA, the Providence Preservation Society and RI Monthly magazine. He is also a recipient of RISD’s John Frazier Award for Excellence in Teaching and of a RISD Alumni Faculty Award. He lives with his wife Victoria in Providence’s Elmwood Historic District.
