Saturday Tour

The Festival’s self-guided tours of private homes on Keene Street and Lloyd Avenue will take place from 10:00 am – 4:00 pm on Saturday, June 6.

Tickets are $45 in advance, with a $10 discount for Providence Preservation Society members. Tickets will be available on the day of the tour for $60 ($50 for PPS members).

Tickets go on sale on April 10

All Festival attendees must check in at the Registration Center on the day of the tour, located at the First Church of Christ, Scientist (71 Prospect Street). Registration opens at 9:45 am and homes open at 10:00 am.

Each guest will receive a Festival map, which will serve as the admission to homes on the tour. Information on the homes will be posted outside each tour stop, and will also be available on the mobile-friendly Festival website.

* Please note that as the COVID-19 situation develops, this event may be postponed until later in 2020. We will update all attendees as information becomes available

Preview Party

A special Festival fundraiser will take place on Thursday, June 4 at the historic 1852 Edwin Halsey Reynolds House. Tickets are available for $125, and include a complimentary ticket to Saturday’s house tours.
