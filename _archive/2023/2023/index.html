Welcome!

PPS’s Festival of Historic Houses Digital Guidebook

Guest check-in and restrooms are located at the Registration Center (Paul Cuffee Upper School / 544 Elmwood Avenue)

  • Your guidemap is your ticket to enter all open properties.
  • Properties are open from 10 am – 4 pm.
  • All properties are within walking distance; some properties may have steep stairs or uneven surfaces.
  • Indoor photography and/or videography, smoking, eating, drinking, and cell phone conversations are prohibited inside the properties.
  • To protect flooring, high-heeled shoes may not be worn inside the properties; Homeowners may request shoe removal or use of shoe covers.
  • Facemasks are welcome but not required.
  • We welcome children over the age of 10, and small children if they are carried
  • Free parking is available on the surrounding streets; please obey all posted signage.
