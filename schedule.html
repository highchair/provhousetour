Program Schedule

Thank you for your interest in attending programs that are part of PPS’s Festival of Modern Design. Each program is ticketed separately, with registration links below each individual program description.

If you are not (yet) a member of Providence Preservation Society and plan to attend more than one event during modernism week, consider joining PPS at the individual new-member rate of $35 for the year or the household rate of $75 for the year! See our membership page for more information!

Modernism Week Pass

Purchase a Modernism Week Pass! A Pass gives you entry to the following programs and events listed below. It does not include entry to events with a capacity of 30 or less – those walking tours and field trips must be booked individually to ensure we do not register beyond capacity. Modernism Week Passes are only available for purchase through October 1.

Included in a Modernism Week Pass:

  • October 5: Keynote Address and Opening Reception, Designing Home: Jews and Midcentury Modernism
  • October 8: Collecting Modern
  • October 9: Mid Mod Cocktail Party
  • October 10: Providence Modern (1945-1995): Architecture, Design, Planning, Culture
  • October 11: Providence Modern Open House

Pass Prices: $325 PPS Members / $375 General

Purchase your Pass on Event Brite

Note: Modernism Week Passes may sell out before October 1 if any of the programs reach capacity.
