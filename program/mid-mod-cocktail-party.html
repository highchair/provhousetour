Thursday, October 9

Mid Mod Cocktail Party

Enjoy cocktails and light bites in a fabulous East Side home designed by Castellucci Galli in 1959. Retro, mid-century or mod attire welcome but not required! The evening includes a silent auction with opportunities to bid on items like a private tour of the Gropius House (1938) in Lincoln, Massachusetts.

Thursday, October 9 | 5:30 – 7:30pm

Ticket Price: $150 PPS Members / $165 General

The Mid Mod Cocktail Party benefits PPS’s year-round efforts to document, interpret, preserve and advocate for Providence’s architectural landmarks and beloved places. Registration for this event will close on October 4.
